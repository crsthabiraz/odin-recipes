Ingredients for VEG MOMO

  • Flour
  • Onion
  • salt and so on.

Ingredients for NON-VEG MOMO

  • Meat of your preference (can be Buff or Chicken).
  • Flour
  • salt
  • other seasonings and so on.

Directions for making MOMO

  1. Heat the MOMO pot.
  2. Add water to it.
  3. Let water boil.
  4. make a flour patty.
  5. wrap the patty with your prefered meat.
  6. Finally, Steam the wrapped dumpling and wait.
  7. In 15 minutes, the dish is ready to be served.
